Wind Speed


Significant changes in latitude affect wind speeds. For example, regions near the equator are characterized by low winds, while mid-latitudes experience much faster airflow. The near absence of land in the southern hemisphere at latitudes of about 40 degrees south is the reason behind the band of strong winds at these latitudes, commonly called the “Roaring Forties.”
